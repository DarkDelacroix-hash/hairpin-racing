The Season

Multi-Class Season 3 · In Progress

GR1 and GR2, same grid, every lap.
The championship is decided in two rounds.

Eight teams have been racing since February across ten iconic circuits. Sully leads individually with 168 points. In the team standings it's razor close at the top: Team Sully-Sully (203 pts) edges Team Boltus-Zerpa (202 pts) by a single point heading into Round 9 at Autopolis Full Course.

02

How It Works

01
Teams of Two
Eight teams, two drivers each. Every race night, both teammates are on track simultaneously — one in GR1, one in GR2.
02
Points Stack
Standard GT7 scoring within each class. The team's score each round is the sum of both drivers' finishing points.
03
Class Flexibility
Partners can swap GR1 and GR2 assignments race to race. Who drives what is part of the strategy.
